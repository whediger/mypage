In The Future...
the only thing that is certain is change and…
we are living during the birth
of the Information Age
Computers are transforming
the way we interact with everything
The way we interact with computers will change
in some cool but somewhat predictable ways.
But as a technology matures…
It’s form and function merge until
there is no difference between the two and
we are able to use it on a completely intuitive level
because the interface is our natural environment.
There are now computers you can swallow to monitor your health.
In our lifetime we will interact with machines
that appeal to our human nature…
and need to solve moral dilemmas
humans have never faced before
Of course, in the future there will still be trees…
In Fact...
There may be more trees than there
has been in thousands of years.
Like it or not…
